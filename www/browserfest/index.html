BrowserFest borrows the playful and dynamic demoparty format to celebrate
and experiment with one of the most flexible and inspiring tools ever
invented: the humble web browser. We rely on web browsers every day,
yet they are often forgotten. They are our gateway to the Internet–and
during COVID-19, they have been our window to the exterior world.
Browsers enable us to read and write the metamedium of the web.

BrowserFest invites individuals, teams and crews of makers, tinkerers,
hackers, poets, musicians, and artists to build demos, sketches, and
artworks that are in and of the browser. Workshops and self-guided group
work will culminate into a ruckus of wild executables, tips, tricks, and
glitch sharing, prizes, awards, and realtime performances.
