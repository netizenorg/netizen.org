BrowserFest launches on Feb 22 🚀
register to receive nfo && updates

BrowserFest is an online competition + celebration of creative coding. it borrows from the playful && dynamic demoparty format to foster the artful development of + experimentation with the WWWeb. over the course of 7 weeks beginning on Feb 22, participants + crews will explore creative coding through live sessions, interactive tutorials, && starter (code) packs, culminating in a realtime interactive event in ur WWWeb browser on April 10.

BrowserFest invites individuals, teams and crews of makers, tinkerers, hackers, poets, musicians, and artists to build demos, sketches, and artworks that are in and of the browser. Workshops and self-guided group work will culminate into a ruckus of wild executables, tips, tricks, and glitch sharing, prizes, awards, and realtime performances.
