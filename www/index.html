netizen.org is a collective of new media artists, educators and activists on a mission to reestablish human agency in the information age through public events, workshops, digital tools and online programming. Geolocated in Chicago, Illinois—at the intersection of digital literacy and Internet art—netizen.org transforms users into netizens, who are critically engaged citizens of the Net.

Our next (online) event is Bias Out of the Box: Unpacking Algorithms of Power; we're co-hosting an online screening of the film Coded Bias followed by a virtual Q&A with director/producer Shalini Kantayya. The following day we'll be co-hosting a virtual convo with algorithmic activists Brandi Geurkink (mozilla) and Freddy Martinez (Lucy Parsons Labs). Hope you can join us!
